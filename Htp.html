How to Play Blackjack

Blackjack is a card game where the goal is to get as close to 21 points as possible without exceeding it. Here’s how you can play:

  • Card Values:
    • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
    • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are each worth 10 points.
    • Ace is worth either 1 or 11 points, depending on what benefits your hand.
  • Objective: The goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. If you exceed 21, you lose!
  • Starting the Game: You’ll receive two cards, and the dealer gets two cards (one face-up). You’ll decide whether to hit (take a card), stand (keep your current hand), double down (double your bet for one more card), or split (split your hand if you have two identical cards).
  • Winning: - If your total is closer to 21 than the dealer’s, you win! - If you get 21 on your first two cards (Ace + 10), it’s a Blackjack and wins automatically! - If the dealer busts (goes over 21), you win as well.
